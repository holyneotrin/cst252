How to make Spam Musubi

  1. First get your ingredients
    Spam, white rice, soy sauce, sugar, large seaweed sheets, water, and furikake seasoning.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. Cut up spam into 1cm slices, long ways.
  4. Add splash of oil into pan. Fry spam on medium heat and drain the oil after.
  5. While pan is still hot add soy sauce and sugar mixture (2:1). Keep on pan until carmelized. Place in a small dipping bowl.
