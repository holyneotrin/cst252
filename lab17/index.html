Hangman OOP

  1. One player thinks of a word and the rest of the players guess the word one letter at a time.[Player]
  2. The chosen player writes a number of dashes equal to the word length [Player + Hangman]
  3. If the guessing player gets a letter correct, the writing player fills out the letter in the correct spots [Player + Hangman]
  4. If the letter is not in the word, the writing player draws a pieve of the hangman's gallows. [Player + Hangman]
  5. Once the hangman's gallows is fully completed, the game ends. [Hangman]
  6. The first player to guess the correct word, gets to choose the next word. [Hangman]
